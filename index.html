About Art House Café

Born from a Love of Art & Nature

"About ten years ago, I noticed that a lot of beautiful furniture in good condition was abandoned as waste. So I decided to create a place dedicated to contribute to nature."

— HE Khalid Sedeeq Al Mutawaa, Chairman

  • Every piece of furniture crafted from recycled & upcycled materials
  • Hand-crafted food and beverages with sourced ingredients
  • A creative, relaxed space for families, friends & professionals
  • Catering service available for any occasion
  • Paint activities while you wait — Eat, Create, Enjoy
