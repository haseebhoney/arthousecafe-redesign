Enjoy a unique creative activity while you dine — or make it the whole experience. Perfect for families, couples, friends and team outings.

Paint a Plate
Personalise your own ceramic plate with our full range of paints and brushes. Once you're done, we fire it in the kiln and you take it home as a one-of-a-kind keepsake. Perfect for kids, couples and creative souls.

👥 All ages

Write a message, draw something meaningful, or simply express yourself on one of our café's bricks. Your art stays on the wall permanently — becoming part of the Art House story forever.

👥 All ages
